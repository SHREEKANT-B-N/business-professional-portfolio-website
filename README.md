## 👤 About

Nikhil Kamath's story defies the conventional path to success. Born in Shimoga, Karnataka, he was raised in Bengaluru in a family with a diverse background — his father a Canara Bank executive and his mother a multi-faceted entrepreneur who managed major corporate events and undertook landscaping projects.

Kamath dropped out of school after the 10th grade, never completing a formal degree. Instead, he started working at a call centre while quietly teaching himself equity trading — an unconventional education that laid the foundation for everything that followed.

At just 20, he became a sub-broker in 2006, teaming up with his brother Nithin to manage high-net-worth portfolios. Four years later, they co-founded **Zerodha** — a company that fundamentally disrupted how India invests. Today, Kamath sits on Forbes' list of India's 100 Richest with a net worth of **$3.3 billion** as of December 2025.

## 🏢 Ventures

### 📈 Zerodha *(Founded 2010)*
India's largest retail stockbroker by active clients. Disrupted the brokerage industry with a flat-fee, discount model — enabling a new generation of retail investors to participate in markets affordably.
`Fintech · Brokerage`

### 💎 True Beacon *(Founded 2020)*
Boutique asset management firm managing investments for ultra-high-net-worth individuals through privately pooled vehicles. Focuses exclusively on the Indian markets with sophisticated, data-driven strategies.
`Asset Management`

### 🌿 Gruhas *(Founded 2021)*
Venture firm co-founded with Abhijeet Pai investing in 50+ startups across climate tech, consumer brands, sustainability, PropTech, and senior care. Operates the Gruhas Collective Consumer Fund and the Earth Fund in partnership with Brigade Group.
`Venture Capital · Impact`

🤝 Philanthropy — The Giving Pledge

In June 2023, Nikhil Kamath signed The Giving Pledge— a commitment by some of the world's wealthiest individuals to dedicate the majority of their wealth to charitable causes. Kamath pledged to donate **50% of his lifetime wealth**, joining an elite global cohort that includes Warren Buffett and Bill Gates.

His philanthropic vision focuses on areas where capital can create systemic, long-term change:

- 🌍 Climate Change & Environmental Sustainability
- 📚 Access to Quality Education
- 🏥 Healthcare & Public Health
